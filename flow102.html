What's a Portfolio Website

A portfolio website is a website that describes a brand or idea to potential paying customers. A portfolio website tells the user what to expect from the brand and sometimes what separates a paying customer from a regular lead is am attractive website.

With this in mind, there's some rules to making sleek, powerful and attractive websites and we shall be sharing some of them and give you the best way to design portfolio websites.

There are two main types of portfolio websites: Corporate or Personal and the way a portfolio website is structured, will depend on the type of website. 

A Corporate website is a website built for a corporate body or entity usually a real company. While a Personal website is one built for a personal brand, like maybe for someone who's a graphics designer or a single freelancer. Note that a small group of people that offer a targeted solution can be treated as a corporate body.  

  1. Menu: A portfolio website must have a menu tab and the content should vary depending on the type. If you have a one-paged website you're building then the menu should contain important pointers of that page such as Home, About, Contact, Testimonials, Pricing etc. Then a multi paged menu should contain links to other pages in your directory.
  2. Hero or Header: In Mobirise web development, the hero section is actually the header section and it's generally the very first thing that the users will see upon loading the site. It's meant to contain the name of the brand and a picture(s) and/or videos about the brand's services.
  3. Features: The features block in a portfolio website can be used to show the services that the business offers as well or specifying that things that the brand has that sets it apart.
  4. Images & Videos: The importance of having images on your site can't be over-emphasized, you may use the inbuilt image generator feature where necessary but if you can get real pictures that relate to the brand directly, it would be very useful.
  5. C.T.A: Call To Action or CTA is a way of telling the users to do something, If you wish users to chat on WhatsApp with the brand or if they wish to set a meeting, you may use free tools like Calendly.
  6. People: In most some cases, the brand may be made up of a collection of individuals and while designing the portfolio, it's important that said important figures be mentioned
  7. Blogs: Yes, it's a modern era and not many people care for blogs but when building portfolios, adding a couple of articles actually adds a bit of authenticity and creates a sense of user awareness whether or not users read them ðŸ˜ª

Key things to remember when building websites

Brand Color

In a portfolio or any project you're building, it's very important to never deviate from the color's of the brand. 

Client

If your client is a single individual or a brand that does one thing, a single page portfolio will suffice but a company portfolio may require multi pages and may even need legal documents!

Font

Although we haven't talked a lot about fonts, understand that for some level of visual aesthetics, you may need to decorate the font of some texts on websites or even change the entire font scheme of the website.

Niche

If you're building a website for a place of worship, school or hospital and the theme is black, you're making a mistake. Certain colors are meant for certain types of websites.

Block format

Mobirise offers a lot of blocks and you may have to improvise and use a block for something other than what it was initially meant for. That's very OK just ensure you apply caution in adding blocks.

Challenge

Meet with people in your area who own a business and find out if they would like a portfolio website and listen for their specifications.
