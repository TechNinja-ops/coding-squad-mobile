What actually is a Blog?

A blog is ideally a collection of write ups about a particular niche. It's also a way for the users or customers of a brand to be able to know what goes on in the brand. 

When designing a blog page, there are two general things to build. The home page which contains all the blogs and then the individual blog page which contains articles. The individual blog pages could be as many as the blogs available are

Being very honest, with the advent of video materials, users don't actually like to read blogs these days unless the brand is very established. Anyway, for the purpose of this lecture, we are going to be classifying blogs into two categories namely portfolio blogs and expression blogs.

A portfolio blog is a blog that is attached to a portfolio website and as such the content of the blog posts are actually very little and are usually tied to whatever service the brand is rendering. An expression blog is a blog that usually has more than one niche and the articles have no limit. An expression blog can can also exist on it's own while a portfolio blog has to exist within a portfolio.

Here are some differences between the two:

Portfolio Blog

  • Found on portfolio websites

  • Best practice is 3 - 8 blog articles 

  • Articles are limited to what happens in the company or brand

  • May not require user feedback (liking and comment)

  • Not very suitable for running commercial intended ads

  • Quick to set up and maintain

  • Home page can contain horizontal scroll

    • Expression Blog

  • Can exist as an independent site

  • No limit on number of articles

  • Articles are best limited to a specific niche or niches

  • Usually requires user feedback

  • Suitable for ads

  • May be easy to setup but maintenance as time goes becomes hard

  • Home page is usually vertically scrolled

    • Building the ideal Portfolio Blog...

    1. Blog Block: The first thing to do is to create the blog block. The blog blocks are put under the "news" block elements. The portfolio page is also the home page of the blog of the portfolio.
    2. Customize The Block: The next thing to do is to customize the block by adding the blog post title, a short description as well as a link to get to the main blog post.
    3. Blog Post: The blog post itself should contain the post name in bold followed by the main cover photo of the post. The best practice for blog posts is to have at least two more images every now and then between paragraphs.
    4. Merge And Upload: You will learn the best practice for updating website content for multi paged websites.

    Building the ideal Expression Blog...

    1. Home Page: The home page of an expression blog should contain the blog's name and possibly a few important values of the blog. Then the blog's articles can follow suit. For best results add upload dates to the blog. You may arrange the blogs in group of three's on larger displays and then single blocks on smaller display.
    2. Main Blog Page: The main blog page should contain the main title of the page and probably a cover photo behind the main title, (though not completely necessary). The body of the article should ideally contain about three pictures strategically located on different parts of the page. The pictures should be set to underscore a salient part of the page. It's important to note that the cover photo on the home page should be the same with the main blog page.
    3. Feedback: You may need to have a part where users can give feedback about the article. This would require some technical skills such as authentication, user state and management, database management and API integrations.     Key things to remember when building websites

    Brand Color

    In a portfolio or any project you're building, it's very important to never deviate from the color's of the brand. 

    Client

    If your client is a single individual or a brand that does one thing, a single page portfolio will suffice but a company portfolio may require multi pages and may even need legal documents!

    Font

    Although we haven't talked a lot about fonts, understand that for some level of visual aesthetics, you may need to decorate the font of some texts on websites or even change the entire font scheme of the website.

    Niche

    If you're building a website for a place of worship, school or hospital and the theme is black, you're making a mistake. Certain colors are meant for certain types of websites.

    Block format

    Mobirise offers a lot of blocks and you may have to improvise and use a block for something other than what it was initially meant for. That's very OK just ensure you apply caution in adding blocks.
