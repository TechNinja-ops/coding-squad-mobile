The Mobirise AI...

Having a developer's block is a ritual you will have to face especially as a frontend dev! We took that into consideration when developing the platform. With the Mobirise AI you'll get a pretty decent head-start when building your websites and modify along the way.

To build with AI, if it's the first time you're opening the app, then simply click the create with AI option. If you're currently on a project, simply hover on the three horizontal menu bar and click sites to get to the home page.

The idea is you provide a detailed explanation of what you're building. Remember Garbage in Garbage out. Luckily for you, we have provided a scheme you can use to get the best out of the AI 

  • Start with the name of the site you're building, that is the name of the project. If the business has some sort of tagline, you may add it here.
  • Now explain the goal of the business. Explain the intention of the business or project which you should get from your client. Ensure you put your mandate and vision. The AI will arrange it for you accordingly.
  • The next thing you'd want to explain is the services you want to include on the site. Put out what problem the Client is suggesting to address. If you're building a church website, things like Youth formation and empowerment, Promoting healthy religion, Provisioning a safe community for growth... Things like that.
  • Next is to point out some important people in your company and what they do. The way you describe them will determine the best block the AI will choose for you.
  • If the business wishes to have their pricing discussed, you can add it here. When adding pricing with relation to packages, it's best to use the enter key for proper pagination. Clearly put the package name and benefits.
  • Last step is to add where the business is located, your working hours as well as contact details such as phone number(s) and or emails
